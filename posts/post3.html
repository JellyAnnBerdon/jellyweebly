Chasing the Sunset

Published on May 2025

There’s something magical about watching the sun slowly dip below the horizon. One evening, I took a moment to pause and appreciate the sunset — the sky was painted with shades of orange, pink, and gold, and for a few minutes, everything felt peaceful and still.


I sat in silence, just observing, letting the breeze hit my face while listening to the distant sounds of the world settling down. It was a reminder to slow down, breathe, and be present.


Moments like these are simple, but they stay with you. Sometimes, all you need is a good view and a quiet mind to end your day right.
